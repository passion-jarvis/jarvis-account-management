Core Philosophy

A monthly email is not client success. It's a courtesy. Client success means the client is getting the outcome they paid for — time back, tasks handled, business growing.

If a client doesn't reply, that is not "all good." No response = yellow flag. Always.

The goal of this SOP is simple: no client should ever be surprised enough to send a formal cancellation email. Every cancellation means we failed weeks before. Catch it early, fix it fast, or escalate immediately.

Your job is not to send emails. Your job is to make clients successful.

Phase 3 — Health Score System
Scoring
Client Health Score Definitions
Updated monthly — or whenever a trigger event occurs
Score Definition Trigger Criteria Action Required
GREEN Client is active, responsive, and getting value Replied within 5 days. No complaints. VA performing. Monthly email only. Ask for referral at 60+ days.
YELLOW Client is disengaged or mildly dissatisfied No reply in 5+ days. Slow responses. Vague complaints. Any mention of dissatisfaction. Ruth calls within 48 hours. No exceptions.
RED Client is at high risk of cancellation No response 14+ days. Formal complaint on record. Mentioned canceling. Formal cancel email received. Ruth + Passion call same day. Escalate immediately.
⚠️

Hard Rule: Zero clients should end the month at Red without Passion being notified. If a client goes Red and Passion doesn't know within 24 hours, that is a failure of this SOP.
